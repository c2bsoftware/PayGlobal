  • You can then take payments whenever there is sufficient mobile coverage.
  • It can handle every type of card.

HOW CAN THIS PAYMENT METHOD HELP YOUR BUSINESS?

This type of payment is ideal for any business that is out on the road and is required to take payment from customers. It is often used by tradesmen or even delivery companies. It is completely secure, takes seconds for payments to be authorised, and there is no need to be linked to a phone line or broadband connection.
