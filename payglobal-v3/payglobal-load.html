PayGlobal Load Balancing

At PayGlobal, our aim is your success. As well as our successful and reliable online marketing services, we can offer you optional services which can contribute to your long-term success. By offering load balancing payment gateway processing between more than one merchant account, we can help you monitor transactions, track your sales volume, mitigate risk and reduce your number of chargebacks. This could be especially useful if you have multiple product lines, high sales volumes, high transaction numbers, multiple sales URLs or merchant account processing limits, as it can allow load balancing between multiple merchant accounts.

By using Transaction Load Balancing for credit and debit cards, as well as for echecks, you can divide your transaction volume across all of your merchant accounts. Using Fast Charge SubIDs under a single account ID means that your transactions will be evenly distributed across your selected SubIDs. This allows you to direct transactions either to all SubIDs, or only to those of your choice.

Benefits of Load Balancing Payment Gateway

  • Spread transactions over several merchant accounts
  • Mitigate risk and reduce chargebacks
  • Use only one payment gateway login to monitor all merchant accounts
  • Single integration for all payment methods

How load balancing works:

Imagine a merchant has four different merchant accounts with four different banks:

  • Merchant Account “A” – 25%
  • Merchant Account “B” – 25%
  • Merchant Account “C” – 25%
  • Merchant Account “D” – 25%

As you can see, this load balancing allows the merchant to spread their transactions evenly across the four different merchant accounts with the four different banks. As merchants often process on a merchant account with lower rates and then switch to another account once they reach their limits, using our Fast Charge service makes life simpler- you’ll only manage and pay for one payment gateway account, no matter which merchant account you use.

PayGlobal’s load balancing feature is ideal for high volume, high risk merchants who need a system with multiple merchant accounts and multiple MIDs for back-up. By using our service, you’ll always have the capacity to generate additional sales volume and prevent payment delays and customer inconvenience.

Our credit card load balancing is easy both to set up and to use.
