More countertop features

Find out more about our countertop card terminal.

  • Install in seconds

    The terminal is ready to use straight out of the box. Just plug it in and follow the simple onscreen instructions.

  • Offer more ways to pay

    Our card machine accept Visa, MasterCard and American Express, allowing customers to pay in any way that want.

  • Serve customers faster

    The telium processor in this card machine can process a payment in less than 2 seconds, so you can serve more customers, quicker.

  • Always stay connected

    The terminal can connect to both the phone and broadband. If you lose internet connection in one, you’ll always have a backup you can rely on.

  • Quick-loading receipt printer

    Replacing till roll couldn’t be easier. Pop open the top of terminal, drop in the paper, and you’re ready to go.

  • Cut queues with contactless

    Contactless lets customers pay with a quick tap of their card. It’s perfect for any business with an average transaction value of £20 or less
